About Me

As someone coming from logging culture, steept in the idea that there is no more noble pursuit than to build something from scratch; to live in the arena that resides on the intersection of colorful art making and rigid engineering, I find the world of web design, and the pursuit of keeping up to an ever-changing environment inherit in it, to feel more like home than work. Pursuing a undergraduate degree in Applied Mathematics at the University of California - San Diego, I have learned the seer joy that comes from new and challenging concepts. As a network analyst in the Army, I learned that projects depend on a vast variety of ideas. To constrict it to one person's ideas would be to constrict a sunflower to soil that only has one nutrient. My time working in the university's IT help desk allowed me to see that while the problem may be common, how someone views it and later describes it will be unique to every person. Working there allowed to work on my ability to understand common problems from every angle so I could better help the customer.
